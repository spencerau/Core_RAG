# Chapman University 2025–2026 Undergraduate Catalog  
## Computer Science, B.S.

## Program Learning Outcomes
- Earn an overall GPA of 2.500 for all required lower-division major courses  
- Earn an overall GPA of 2.000 for all required major courses  
- Complete all courses with a grade of C- or higher (except P/NP courses)  
- Complete at least 21 upper-division credits in the major  

## General Education Requirement
- FFC 100B – First Year Foundations: Grand Challenges in Science and Engineering  

## Grand Challenges Initiative (3 credits)

### GCI 150 – Grand Challenges I (1 credit)
- Mentored research seminar  
- Focus on critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication  
- Cannot be taken concurrently with GCI 200 or GCI 250  

### GCI 200 – Grand Challenges II (1 credit)
- Prerequisite: GCI 150  
- Continuation of mentored research experience  
- Cannot be taken concurrently with GCI 150 or GCI 250  

### GCI 250 – Grand Challenges III (1 credit)
- Prerequisites: GCI 150 and GCI 200  
- Advanced continuation of research  
- Cannot be taken concurrently with GCI 150 or GCI 200
